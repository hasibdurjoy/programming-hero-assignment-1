History

From 1984 to present

Dinner at Food world was a wildly popular mystery novel by the once-celebrated author Frances Parkinson Keyes that appeared in 1948. The first scene depicts the crowds waiting outside the restaurant, which took reservations only for its socially prominent regulars. The opening scene is a dinner party held in the glowing, red “1840 Room” starting a complicated story of passion and betrayal. So familiar was the restaurant to the American public that the concise title needed no elaboration.
